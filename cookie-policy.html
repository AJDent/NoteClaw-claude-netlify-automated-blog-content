Cookie Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Take Notes Capital uses cookies and similar technologies on its blog and related pages.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, understand traffic, and improve performance.

2. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential cookies: Needed for core site functionality.
  • Performance and analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the blog and which pages perform best.
  • Functional cookies: Remember settings or choices you make.
  • Marketing cookies: May be used to measure campaign performance or support remarketing, if enabled.

3. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve site experience, analyze traffic, measure content performance, support forms and user sessions, and understand what topics visitors engage with most.

4. Third-Party Tools

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services used for analytics, scheduling, forms, or embedded content. Those providers may process information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can usually control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect site functionality and the quality of your experience.

6. Updates

We may revise this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

7. Contact

For questions about cookies or tracking on this site, contact us through the information provided at https://takenotescapital.com.
